RESTAURAN

DISFRUTAR

Open since December 2014, the restaurant Disfrutar (in English “Enjoy”) arose after years of intense work at elBulli, where Mateu Casañas, Oriol Castro and Eduard Xatruch met and trained professionally, and after reaffirming the work done at Compartir opened in April 2012 in Cadaqués.

DISFRUTAR BARCELONA

The restaurant

Located in the Eixample, just in front of the Ninot Market, the premises are also surprising due to their design. Respect for the history and legacy of the Mediterranean are the inspiration and ceramic is the common theme marking the interior. Gastronomic proposal

In Barcelona the gastronomic offer revolves around the tasting menu, with clearly avant-garde cuisine and where the dishes stand out for their great, markedly Mediterranean identity. In short, a restaurant with daring, fun and modern cuisine searching for taste as the main proposition.

Concept and gastronomy

Creative emotion as a fundamental argument of gastronomy is the DNA of Disfrutar.

To be able to move it in a personalized way, in all kinds of places and adapting to each event and each person, Disfrutar has achieved, through its creative potential applied specially from its culinary line, not only faithfully translating its elaborations into this new format, but amplify the essential flavours, the “wow” textures, the fun in each bite, the surprise in each presentation. With formats and staging for any situation. Pure emotion.
